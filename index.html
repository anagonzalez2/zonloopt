Our Vision

When working on our final project at the 2019 Kode with Klossy Web Development camp, we realized that many people, especially women, face several dangers when running outside on their own in an unfamiliar area. We’ve continued working on our project, Zon Loopt: Running Routes, in an effort to solve this problem by providing an app designed to generate safe running routes based on location input and desired distance. We believe that everyone deserves to run without fear. Our mission is to empower you so that running can still be a fun and joyful experience. Our hope is that Zon Loopt will give you the peace of mind to do so.

Zon Loopt means "the sun is running" in Dutch. Our mission is to encourage users to get out, explore, and run in the sunshine while traveling- not stay cooped up in the hotel gym. And we'll always loop you right back to your starting point.



We will start with operating on iOS with plans to expand to Android in the future. You will soon be able to send the route to your watch face and run from there also.
